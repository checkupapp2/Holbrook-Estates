Flathead Valley Living

Where natural beauty meets custom home luxury

Holbrook Estates is a premier residential development located in the heart of Flathead Valley in Kalispell, MT. Surrounded by the unmatched beauty of Glacier National Park, Whitefish Mountain Ski Resort and Flathead Lake, this exclusive community offers 1-2 acre build-to-suit lots designed for custom home living.

With a total of 35 lots, 12 of the 29 lots remaining offer the rare opportunity of a secondary residence up to 2,000 square feet. Set in a "park-like" setting with paved roads, our community features 2 plentiful water wells plus a separate fire well with backup generator for complete reliability.

Prime Location

Perfectly positioned for luxury living

Glacier National Park

45 minutes away - A national park encompassing more than 1 million acres and includes parts of two mountain ranges, more than 130 named lakes, more than 1,000 different species of plants, and hundreds of species of animals. This vast, pristine ecosystem is the centerpiece of the "Crown of the Continent Ecosystem".

Flathead Lake

30 minutes south - Located 7 miles south of Kalispell, Flathead Lake is approximately 30 miles long and 16 miles wide, covering 197 square miles. It has a maximum depth of 370.7 ft and lies in a scenic part of Montana, flanked by two scenic highways.

Whitefish Mountain Resort

30 minutes away - Located 35 miles west of Glacier National Park with over 3,000 skiable acres, making it one of the largest ski resorts in the U.S. and Canada. Offers state-of-the-art grooming, snowmaking, lifts, skiing, snowboarding, snowshoeing, and guided snowmobile rentals.
